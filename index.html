About CMN

Central Mindanao Newswatch (CMN) is a dedicated news and media platform committed to delivering accurate, timely, and relevant information across Mindanao and beyond. Rooted in the principles of responsible journalism, CMN serves as a reliable source of truth in an age of rapid information exchange.

Beyond digital news delivery, CMN continues to uphold the tradition of news printing ensuring that communities without consistent internet access remain informed. Through carefully curated print editions, we bring verified stories, public service information, and local updates directly to readers.

Our team of writers, editors, and field reporters work tirelessly to gather facts, verify sources, and present news with integrity. Whether through our mobile application or printed publications, CMN bridges the gap between technology and traditional media.

At CMN, we believe that access to truthful information empowers communities, strengthens democracy, and inspires progress.
